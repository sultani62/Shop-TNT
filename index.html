About Sunblock Window Tint, LLC

Orangevale's Premier Tinting Specialists

With over a decade of experience in the automotive and residential glass tinting industry, Sunblock Window Tint, LLC has established itself as Orangevale's premier choice for premium window film solutions.

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality materials from industry-leading manufacturers like 3M, Llumar, and Suntek. Our certified technicians are passionate about delivering flawless installations that exceed industry standards.

From luxury vehicles to commercial buildings, we provide customized tinting solutions that enhance privacy, reduce heat by up to 60%, protect against 99% of harmful UV rays, and elevate the aesthetic appeal of any glass surface.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction ensures that every project meets our rigorous standards of excellence, backed by our comprehensive lifetime warranty.
